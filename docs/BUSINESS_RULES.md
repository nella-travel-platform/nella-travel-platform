# Experience Cancun - Business Rules

## Pricing

### Car Rentals
- Regular daily price applies year-round.
- Seasonal prices override the regular daily price only during their effective dates.
- No regular season date ranges need to be maintained.

### Reservation Deposit
- Customer pays a reservation deposit equal to one day's rental to confirm the booking.
- A reservation is not confirmed until the deposit is received.

### Damage Deposit
- Default damage deposit is 4,000 MXN.
- Larger vehicles may require a higher damage deposit.
- The damage deposit is collected at vehicle pickup.
- The damage deposit is refunded if the vehicle is returned in acceptable condition.

### Tour Pricing

Tour pricing may vary based on:

- Visitor type
  - International Visitor
  - Mexican Resident
  - Quintana Roo Resident
  - Local Promotions

- Traveler age
  - Infant
  - Child
  - Adult
  - Senior

Each tour defines its own age ranges.

Example:

International Adult
International Child

Mexican Adult
Mexican Child

Children under the configured minimum age may receive free admission.

### Seasonal Pricing

Seasonal pricing may apply to:

- Car rentals
- Tours
- Vacation rentals
- Airport transfers

Seasonal prices override regular prices during their effective dates.

### Future Pricing Rules

The pricing engine should support:

- Promotional pricing
- Coupon codes
- Group discounts
- Resident discounts
- Holiday pricing
- Weekend pricing
- Dynamic pricing
- Package pricing
